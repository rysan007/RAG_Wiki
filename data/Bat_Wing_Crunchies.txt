Bat Wing Crunchies are a bizarre but well-known baked snack consumed by adventurers across Norrath. The primary ingredient is bat wings, which are commonly harvested from the various giant bats found in caves and across the Butcherblock Mountains. While evil races often purchase them in bulk as a cheap ration, Magicians also highly value bat wings as a critical spell reagent for casting Levitation and Flare. Baking these crunchies requires access to an oven or a collapsible spit and a fair amount of culinary bravery.
