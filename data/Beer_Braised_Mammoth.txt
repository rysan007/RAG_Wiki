Beer Braised Mammoth is a classic, hearty culinary dish prized by the warriors and tradesfolk of Norrath. The recipe requires thick mammoth steaks, which are typically harvested from the massive beasts roaming the frozen tundras of Everfrost. The meat is then slow-cooked and braised in a robust tavern ale or dwarven beer, creating a stat-boosting meal that fortifies an adventurer's stamina against the freezing elements. It is an advanced baking recipe that requires a high tradeskill trivial level to perfect.
