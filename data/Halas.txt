Halas is a harsh, frozen city located in the northern reaches of Antonica, serving as the ancestral home of the barbarian northmen. The city is fiercely traditional, ruled by a council of elders and devoted to the worship of the Tribunal. Due to the lacerating cold of Everfrost Peaks, only the hardiest professions thrive here, including warriors of the Pit of Doom, shamans, rogues, and beastlords. Visitors and locals alike must travel across the frigid waters surrounding the city using a legendary raft known as The Gwenavyne.
