Rivervale is a peaceful village nestled in the mountains bordering the Misty Thicket on the continent of Antonica. It serves as the starting city and haven for the mischievous halflings of Norrath. The town is protected by the Great Wall of Serillis and governed by Mayor Gubbin, a former rogue who is famously wealthy. Despite its tranquil farms, bustling docks run by Fiddy Bobick, and numerous guildhalls, Rivervale is occasionally targeted by goblin and orc raiders from the surrounding wilderness.
