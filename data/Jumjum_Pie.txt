Jumjum pie is a popular handcrafted food item in the world of Norrath, heavily associated with the halflings of Rivervale. It is made from Jumjum, a sweet and highly sought-after crop grown locally in the region's farms. Consuming a Jumjum pie increases a traveler's health regeneration rate when they are resting outside of combat. It is considered a Tier 2 food and provides satiation for over five hours, making it a reliable staple for adventurers braving the dangers of the Misty Thicket.
